Why route nationwide work through OC Serves

If your firm litigates beyond your home state, you already know the friction: a new vendor to vet in every jurisdiction, different invoices, different formats, and no single person accountable for the result. OC Serves removes that friction.

As a member of the National Association of Professional Process Servers (NAPPS), OC Serves can coordinate service through professional process servers across the country. You hand the assignment to us; we place it with a vetted server in the correct jurisdiction, manage the attempts, and deliver the affidavit back to you.

How Nationwide Service Works

Four steps, one point of contact.

You Submit

Send your documents and the service address. One intake, regardless of how many states are involved.

We Route

We place the assignment with a vetted NAPPS member who serves that specific jurisdiction.

They Serve

A professional process server familiar with local rules makes diligent, documented attempts.

We Return

You receive the completed affidavit — formatted for your venue — through your single OC Serves contact.
