Corporate service of process, handled locally

Serving a corporation is not the same as serving an individual. The documents must reach the entity's designated agent for service of process — or, where permitted, an officer or managing agent at a corporate location. Get the recipient or the address wrong, and the service can be challenged.

OC Serves handles corporate service throughout Orange County and California every business day. We are familiar with the major corporate locations in the region, the front-desk and security procedures at each, and what each venue expects to see on the affidavit.

Many corporations designate a professional registered-agent service company rather than maintaining their own agent. Several of these firms operate right here in Newport Beach, and OC Serves serves them directly.

For corporations whose registered agent sits outside our area, we coordinate service through the NAPPS member network so you still get one point of contact and one affidavit back.
