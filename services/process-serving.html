What process serving means for your case

Service of process is the formal delivery of legal documents that gives a court jurisdiction over a party. Done correctly, it protects your case. Done carelessly, it invites a motion to quash and weeks of delay. OC Serves treats every assignment as if the validity of the case depends on it — because it does.

We work primarily with law firms and other process serving companies, including a large share of out-of-state counsel who need a dependable partner in California.

Hard-to-Serve Subjects

Evasive defendant? That's our specialty.

Some people do not want to be found. We combine persistent, varied attempts with skip tracing to locate and serve subjects who are avoiding service.

When standard attempts are exhausted, we document everything thoroughly so you have a clean record to support a motion for substituted or alternative service.

Our approach to difficult serves

  • Attempts at varied times — mornings, evenings, weekends
  • Skip tracing to locate current addresses
  • Stakeouts where the situation calls for it
  • Detailed declarations of diligence
  • Guidance on when substituted service may apply
