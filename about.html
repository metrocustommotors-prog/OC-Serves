Who we are

OC Serves is a professional process serving company based in Orange County, California. We handle service of process for law firms and for other process serving companies — and a large share of that work comes from out-of-state counsel who need a dependable partner in California and beyond.

Process serving sounds simple until it goes wrong. A document served on the wrong party, at an outdated address, or with a poorly documented affidavit can cost a firm weeks and invite a motion to quash. We built OC Serves around the opposite outcome: diligent attempts, the right recipient, and an affidavit that holds up in the venue where it is filed.

What we focus on

We are not trying to be everything to everyone. Our work concentrates where we are genuinely strong:

  • Out-of-state law firms who need a reliable California partner and a single point of contact.
  • Corporate and registered agent service, including major corporate locations in Orange County we attend regularly.
  • HOA service throughout Orange County — a genuine specialty, with all the entity and board complications that come with it.
  • Nationwide coordination through the NAPPS network, so your firm manages one relationship instead of fifty.
How We Work

The standards behind every serve.

01

Diligence

Varied attempts at varied times, with skip tracing when a subject is evading service. Every attempt is documented.

02

Accuracy

We verify recipients and addresses before attempting service, so effort is not wasted on stale information.

03

Accountability

Our Network

Member of NAPPS.

OC Serves is a member of the National Association of Professional Process Servers, the leading professional organization for the process serving industry.

That membership is what lets a single Orange County company offer genuinely nationwide service. When your matter reaches a jurisdiction outside our area, we route it to a vetted NAPPS member there — and manage the assignment so you never lose your single point of contact.
