How It Works

Four steps from request to affidavit.

Submit

Send documents and service instructions through our intake form, email, or your preferred portal.

Assign

We serve it ourselves locally, or route it to a vetted NAPPS member in the correct jurisdiction.

Serve

Diligent, documented attempts are made promptly, with skip tracing if the subject proves evasive.

Return

You receive a completed, venue-ready affidavit of service — typically delivered electronically.
